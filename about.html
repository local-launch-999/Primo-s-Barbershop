Where It Started

Primo's Barbershop opened its doors in 2009 on Wall Street in downtown Norwalk. What began as a small shop with five dedicated barbers quickly became a go-to grooming destination for the entire community — built on skill, consistency, and a genuine love for the craft.

"You're more than just a customer — you're family."

That wasn't a slogan — it was the standard from day one. Every barber at Primo's is committed to making every client feel genuinely welcome, cared for, and confident when they leave the chair.

The Craft

From precision fades and clean line-ups to beard shaping and scissors work, every service is performed with the kind of attention that only comes from years of hands-on experience. No rushed cuts. No shortcuts. Just consistent, high-quality work — every single visit.

The Community

Over 15 years in, Primo's has become part of the fabric of Norwalk. Fathers bring sons. Regulars come every two weeks. Visitors from out of town find it by reputation and leave as fans. 146+ five-star Google reviews tell the story better than we ever could.

Our Values

01
Craft First

Every cut is taken seriously. We don't rush, we don't cut corners, and we don't settle for close enough. Your hair deserves the full attention of a skilled barber.
