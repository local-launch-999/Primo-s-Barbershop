What We Offer

Our Services

Walk-ins always welcome. Call or text (203) 354-4442 to request your preferred barber.

01 — Haircut
Classic Cut

A precise, tailored cut built around your specific face shape and style preference. Any length, any texture — handled with expert technique and full attention. Whether you have a reference photo or just a general idea, we'll make it work.

All Hair Types↗
02 — Fade
Precision Fade

High, mid, or low — our barbers execute crisp gradient transitions with sharp edging and clean intentional lines from top to bottom. The signature service at Primo's, perfected over 15 years of daily practice.

Signature Style↗
03 — Beard
Beard Trim

Shape, define, and line up your beard for a clean, polished, intentional look. We tighten the edges, clean the neckline, and nail whatever silhouette you're going for — from a tight taper to a full, shaped beard.

Grooming↗
04 — Kids
Kids' Cut

Patient, calm, and experienced with all ages — from toddlers to teenagers. We understand that kids need a different approach, and our barbers have the experience and personality to make the chair a comfortable, even fun experience.

All Ages↗
05 — Custom
Custom Style

Bring a reference photo, a description, or just a vibe — our barbers will work closely with you to understand and execute exactly the style you have in mind. We specialize in making ideas a reality.

Bespoke↗
06 — Scissor
Scissor Cut

Soft, textured finishes using shears for clients who prefer a more natural look with carefully controlled shape and flow. Perfect for longer lengths or anyone who wants a cleaner, softer finish without heavy clipper use.

Textured Finish↗
