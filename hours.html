Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need an appointment?

Nope — walk-ins are always welcome. If you want a specific barber, calling ahead or texting (203) 354-4442 is the best way to make sure they're available.

How do I book a specific barber?

Just call or text us at (203) 354-4442 and let us know which barber you'd like. We'll make sure they're ready for you.

Do you cut all hair types?

Yes — all textures, all types, all genders. Our barbers have experience across the full spectrum and welcome everyone through the door.

Is there parking nearby?

Yes. There's a garage right nearby and street parking along Wall St. You shouldn't have trouble finding a spot.

Do you cut kids' hair?

Absolutely. Our barbers — especially Ivan — are experienced and patient with children of all ages. Toddlers to teenagers, no problem.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and debit. No issues either way — just tip your barber well if they did a great job!
